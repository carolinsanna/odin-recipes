Barley Bread

Ingredients

  • 500g of barley flour
  • 1 tablespoon of active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 400ml of warm water

Preparation

  1. In a large bowl, mix the barley flour, yeast, and salt.
  2. Gradually add the warm water and mix until a homogeneous dough forms.
  3. Cover the bowl with a clean cloth and let the dough rest for about 1 hour, or until it doubles in size.
  4. Preheat the oven to 200°C (390°F).
  5. Place the dough in a greased baking pan and bake for approximately 40 minutes, or until the bread is golden.
  6. Let it cool before slicing and enjoying.
