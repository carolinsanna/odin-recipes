Fish and Vegetable Soup

Ingredients

  • 500g of fresh fish (salmon, cod, or your choice)
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 potatoes, cubed
  • 1 leek, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 liter of fish broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot and saut√© the onion, leek, and carrots for a few minutes.
  2. Add the fish, potatoes, and fish broth. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Let the soup cook over medium heat for about 20 minutes, or until the potatoes are tender.
Serve hot and enjoy!
